6 Days / 5 Nights

Destinations Covered
  • Shimla
  • Manali
  • Kufri
  • Solang Valley
Detailed Itinerary
Day 1: Arrival in Shimla
  • Arrive at Shimla and check in to your honeymoon suite.
  • Spend the evening at leisure, enjoying the cool breeze and stunning views of the Himalayas.
  • Stroll along Mall Road and experience the charm of the town.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.
Day 2: Shimla - Kufri Excursion
  • After breakfast, embark on an exciting excursion to Kufri, known for its picturesque snow-capped peaks.
  • Enjoy snow activities like skiing, horse riding, and a yak ride with your partner.
  • Return to Shimla in the afternoon and visit Jakhoo Temple and The Ridge.
  • Evening: A special candlelight dinner will be arranged for you at the hotel.
  • Overnight stay in Shimla.
Day 3: Shimla to Manali
  • Post breakfast, drive to the scenic town of Manali.
  • En route, visit the beautiful Kullu Valley, known for its river rafting and lush landscapes.
  • Arrive in Manali by evening and check in to your honeymoon suite.
  • Overnight stay in Manali.
Day 4: Manali Sightseeing
  • Start your day with a visit to the ancient Hidimba Devi Temple, surrounded by cedar forests.
  • Explore Vashisht Hot Springs and take a rejuvenating bath.
  • Visit Tibetan Monastery and shop for local souvenirs at Mall Road.
  • Return to your hotel for a romantic evening together.
  • Overnight stay in Manali.
Day 5: Solang Valley Adventure
  • After breakfast, head to Solang Valley, known for its adventure sports and breathtaking views.
  • Enjoy paragliding, zorbing, and a cable car ride with panoramic views of the Himalayas (seasonal activities).
  • Spend some time together amidst nature, capturing beautiful memories.
  • Return to Manali in the evening.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.
Day 6: Departure from Manali
  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel.
  • Departure with unforgettable memories of your romantic honeymoon in the hills.
  • Proceed to your onward journey.
Short Description

Celebrate the beginning of your love story with our Honeymoon Special Shimla-Manali package. Experience breathtaking landscapes, romantic settings, cozy accommodations, and unforgettable moments in the lap of the Himalayas. A perfect blend of relaxation, adventure, and romance awaits you!

Price Details

Starting from ₹30,000 per couple (based on twin sharing)

Accommodation Details

  • Manali: Hotel Snow Valley Resorts or similar (2 Nights)
Inclusions
  • 3 Nights accommodation in Shimla
  • 2 Nights accommodation in Manali
  • Daily breakfast and dinner
  • Candlelight dinner for the couple in Shimla
  • All transfers and sightseeing by private vehicle
  • Excursions to Kufri and Solang Valley
  • Toll taxes, driver charges, and parking fees
Exclusions
  • Airfare/train fare to and from Shimla/Manali

Honeymoon Kashmir Details

Short Summary
Duration

6 Days / 4 Cities

Destinations Covered
  • Srinagar
  • Gulmarg
  • Pahalgam
  • Sonmarg
Detailed Itinerary
Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar
  • Arrive in Srinagar and check in to a luxurious houseboat on Dal Lake.
  • Take a romantic Shikara ride on the lake, surrounded by picturesque views of the Himalayas.
  • Spend the evening at leisure, exploring the local markets or enjoying a sunset by the lake.
  • Overnight stay on the houseboat.
Day 2: Srinagar Sightseeing
  • After breakfast, explore the beauty of Srinagar.
  • Visit the famous Mughal Gardens – Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, and Chashme Shahi, renowned for their architecture and floral beauty.
  • Visit the Hazratbal Shrine and take in the spiritual atmosphere.
  • Enjoy a romantic candlelight dinner in the evening.
  • Overnight stay in Srinagar.
Day 3: Excursion to Gulmarg
  • After breakfast, head to the picturesque hill station of Gulmarg (Meadow of Flowers).
  • Enjoy a Gondola ride (cable car) to witness the snow-clad mountains and stunning views.
  • If visiting during winter, indulge in skiing and other snow activities.
  • Spend the day exploring the natural beauty of Gulmarg with your partner.
  • Return to Srinagar by evening for an overnight stay.
Day 4: Pahalgam Adventure
  • After breakfast, drive to Pahalgam, known for its stunning valleys and lush greenery.
  • Visit Betaab Valley, where Bollywood movies were shot, and take a horse ride with your partner.
  • Explore Aru Valley and Lidder River, offering scenic beauty and peaceful surroundings.
  • Spend the evening enjoying a leisurely walk along the riverside.
  • Return to Srinagar for an overnight stay.
Day 5: Sonmarg Day Trip
  • Post breakfast, proceed for a scenic drive to Sonmarg (Meadow of Gold).
  • Admire the snow-capped peaks and lush meadows as you stroll hand in hand.
  • Enjoy activities like horse riding or take an optional excursion to Thajiwas Glacier.
  • Return to Srinagar by evening for a relaxing night.
  • Overnight stay in Srinagar.
Day 6: Departure from Srinagar
  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel.
  • Depart from Srinagar with wonderful memories of your Kashmir honeymoon.
  • Proceed to your onward journey.
Short Description

Embark on a romantic journey through the breathtaking beauty of Kashmir. With serene lakes, stunning mountains, and enchanting gardens, this honeymoon package offers the perfect mix of adventure, tranquility, and love.

Price Details

Starting from ₹55,000 per couple (based on twin sharing)

Accommodation Details
  • Srinagar : Houseboat on Dal Lake or similar (1 Night)
  • Srinagar : Hotel Grand Palace or similar (2 Nights)
  • Gulmarg :Hotel Khyber Resort or similar (2 Nights)
Inclusions
  • 2 Nights accommodation in Srinagar (Hotel)
  • 1 Night accommodation on a houseboat (Dal Lake)
  • 2 Nights accommodation in Gulmarg
  • Daily breakfast and dinner
  • Shikara ride on Dal Lake
  • Gondola ride in Gulmarg (Phase 1)
  • All transfers and sightseeing by private vehicle
  • Excursions to Pahalgam and Sonmarg
  • Candlelight dinner for the couple
  • All toll taxes, driver allowances, and parking fees
Exclusions
  • Airfare/train fare to and from Shimla/Manali

Short Summary
Duration

50 Days / 49 Nights

Destinations Covered
  • 45 Cities across India (including Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, Goa, Jaipur, Cochin, and more)
Detailed Itinerary
Day 1: Delhi – Start of the Journey
  • Begin your journey from Delhi, exploring iconic spots like India Gate and Humayun's Tomb.
  • Overnight stay in Delhi.
Day 2-4: Jaipur, Udaipur, and Jaisalmer (Rajasthan)
  • Explore the Pink City Jaipur, Udaipur's lakes, and the desert landscapes of Jaisalmer.
  • Experience cultural shows, fort visits, and more.
  • Overnight stays in each city.
Day 5-9: Gujarat & Maharashtra
  • Travel through Ahmedabad, Surat, and Mumbai.
  • Explore Sabarmati Ashram and the bustling streets of Mumbai.
  • Enjoy a Mumbai city tour including the Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and Elephanta Caves.
  • Overnight stays in Surat, Ahmedabad, and Mumbai.
Day 10-12: Goa
  • Discover the beautiful beaches of Goa, along with historic forts and churches.
  • Relax and indulge in Goan cuisine and beach activities.
  • Overnight stay in Goa.
Day 13-18: Karnataka and Kerala
  • Journey through Mangalore, Coorg, and into Kerala's famous backwaters.
  • Visit Cochin, Alleppey, and relax on a houseboat.
  • Explore the lush hills of Munnar.
  • Overnight stays in each city.
Day 19-23: Tamil Nadu
  • Explore the vibrant culture of Tamil Nadu through Chennai, Mahabalipuram, and Pondicherry.
  • Visit historic temples and relax on serene beaches.
  • Overnight stays in Pondicherry and Chennai.
Day 24-30: Andhra Pradesh & Telangana
  • Visit Hyderabad and Visakhapatnam to experience historic forts, palaces, and a vibrant food culture.
  • Explore Ramoji Film City and Araku Valley.
  • Overnight stays in Hyderabad and Visakhapatnam.
Day 31-40: Odisha and West Bengal
  • Discover the heritage temples of Bhubaneswar, the beaches of Puri, and the famous Sun Temple of Konark.
  • In Kolkata, explore Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge, and enjoy a Ganga river cruise.
  • Overnight stays in Bhubaneswar, Puri, and Kolkata.
Day 41-45: Northeast India
  • Journey through Assam, Meghalaya, and Nagaland.
  • Visit Kaziranga National Park, explore Shillong, and experience the local cultures.
  • Overnight stays in Shillong and Guwahati.
Day 46-50: Return to Delhi via Uttar Pradesh and Bihar
  • Visit Varanasi, Lucknow, and Patna.
  • Witness the Ganga Aarti in Varanasi and explore the historical sites of Lucknow.
  • Return to Delhi on Day 50 to conclude your road trip adventure.
  • Overnight stays in Varanasi, Lucknow, and Patna.

Explore the diverse landscapes, cultures, and beauty of India in one epic journey. Spanning across 45 cities, this 50-day adventure covers the best of the Indian peninsula, from bustling metropolises to serene coastal towns. A once-in-a-lifetime experience, priced at ₹2,90,000 per person.
